Day 90-116

Delivery

The final transition from development to production. Engineers focus on automation, operational excellence, and delivering business value to clients.

Phase Description

In the final 18 days, the focus shifts entirely to production readiness. The prototypes built over the previous weeks are hardened, secured, and automated. Engineers learn what it means to be part of a billable, client-facing delivery team.

Key Technologies

  • CI/CD & Automation: GitLab CI, Jenkins, Terraform, and Ansible.
  • Monitoring & SRE: Datadog, Prometheus, Grafana, and incident response.
  • Security: DevSecOps practices and compliance scanning.

Learning Journey

  • Production automation strategies
  • Transitioning to billable engineering teams
  • Operational deployment and runbooks
