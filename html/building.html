Day 60-90

Building

Putting theory into practice through rapid prototyping and intensive hackathons. Engineers build functional solutions for complex enterprise use cases.

Phase Description

During the Building phase, teams are organized into agile pods and tasked with solving real-world business problems. A major focus is placed on the insurance domain, challenging engineers to develop over 40 distinct use cases through rapid, iterative development cycles.

Key Technologies

  • Development Stack: Advanced Python, Node.js, and Java integrations.
  • Data Engineering: ETL pipelines and real-time streaming (Kafka).
  • AI Integration: Embedding predictive models and GenAI into applications.

Learning Journey

  • 40+ insurance use cases
  • Intensive weekend hackathons
  • Rapid prototyping and MVP delivery
